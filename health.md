# Holistic Wellness App - Development Plan

## 🌟 App Features
1. **Smart Onboarding:** User Profile (Name, Age, Gender, Location, Weight, Height, Goals).
2. **Weather-Synced Water Tracker:** Adjusts daily water intake based on local weather and humidity.
3. **Location-Based Skin & Hair Care:** Routine suggestions based on local AQI (pollution), temperature, and humidity.
4. **Weight Balance:** Daily calorie and activity/step goals based on user profile.
5. **Sleep Optimizer:** Sleep tracking and its impact on daily energy and skin.
6. **Cycle Tracker:** Syncing skincare and diet with menstrual cycle phases.
7. **Mood & Stress Journal:** Daily mood logging with quick meditation suggestions.
8. **Habit Builder:** Daily check-ins for vitamins, warm water, and sleep hygiene.
9. **AI Wellness Coach:** A quick chat interface for health queries.
10. **Weekly Progress Report:** Motivation and summary of the week's achievements.
