# 🌟 Holistic Wellness App - Advanced Development Plan

## 📱 Core Features Overview

### 1. Account & Smart Onboarding
- **User Authentication:** Proper Signup, Login, and secure user data storage.
- **Advanced Profile:** Name, Age, Gender (Male, Female, Others).
- **Flexible Units:** Height (cm or feet), Weight (kg or pounds).
- **Goal Setting:** Ask user about their ideal body goals before starting.

### 2. Location & Weather Intelligence
- **Mandatory Location & Temp:** Displays current temperature, weather, and AQI on the dashboard.
- **Dynamic Suggestions:** Real-time lifestyle, food, and skincare suggestions based on the local weather.

### 3. Core Trackers & Gamification
- **Tamagotchi Water Tracker:** A virtual plant that grows when you drink water and wilts if you forget. Synced with local weather for daily water intake goals.
- **Wellness Coins & Rewards:** Earn coins for completing daily tasks (sleep, water, workout) to unlock premium features or discounts.
- **Daily "Spin the Wheel":** A daily mini-challenge (e.g., "Do 10 squats", "Drink 1 extra glass of water") for bonus rewards.
- **Habit Builder:** Daily check-ins for vitamins, sleep hygiene, etc.

### 4. AI Powered Nutrition & Diet
- **Diet Plan Generator:** Personalized meal plans based on user's weight, goals, and weather.
- **Smart Grocery List:** Automatically generate a shopping list from the diet plan.
- **AI Food Photo Scanner:** Upload a picture of food to get its calorie count, health score, and whether it's suitable to eat in the current weather.

### 5. Advanced Exercise & Fitness
- **Targeted Workouts:** Exercises for specific body parts based on the user's initial goal selection.
- **Guided Steps & Timers:** Proper step-by-step guides with time durations for each exercise.
- **Animated 3D Avatar:** A visual representation of the user that gets fitter as they progress towards their goals.
- **Before & After Time-Lapse:** App securely takes daily/weekly photos and generates a progress time-lapse.

### 6. Wellness & Self-Care
- **Advanced Skin & Hair Care:** Highly personalized tips based on the exact local weather, humidity, and AQI.
- **Cycle Tracker:** Syncing diet and gentle exercises with menstrual cycle phases (for Female users).
- **Sleep Optimizer & ASMR:** Sleep tracking paired with bedtime stories, ASMR, and nature sounds.
- **Mood Journal & Spotify Integration:** Logs daily mood and automatically suggests Spotify/Music playlists (e.g., relaxing sounds for rain, upbeat for workout).

### 7. AI Multilingual Chatbot
- **Smart Conversational AI:** An intelligent bot that answers any health/fitness-related question.
- **Auto-Language Detection:** Bot replies in the exact same language (e.g., English, Urdu, Roman Urdu) the user types in.
